# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This project is for constructive, professional collaboration. We want
`vettd` to be a place where people can ask questions, propose changes,
report bugs, and disagree on technical choices without being hostile or
demeaning.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and specific in feedback
- Assume good intent, especially in early discussions
- Focus criticism on ideas, code, and decisions rather than people
- Share context when raising security, product, or architectural concerns
- Accept correction gracefully and help keep threads productive

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Insults, pile-ons, or bad-faith argumentation
- Sexualized language or unwelcome sexual attention
- Doxxing, stalking, or publishing private information
- Repeatedly derailing discussions or disrupting collaboration

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to project spaces, including issues, pull
requests, discussions, review comments, and other official community
channels for `vettd`.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of
Conduct, email **security@agentichighway.ai** with **[Code of Conduct]**
in the subject line.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content, limit participation, or take
other action they believe is necessary to protect the community and keep
discussion productive.
